Life at CrestHill University

Experience a Vibrant Campus Community

Central Library

Over 50,000 books, e-journals, research databases, reading halls, and digital learning resources.

Computer Labs

Modern labs equipped with advanced systems, software, and high-speed internet for practical learning.

Science Labs

Well-equipped laboratories designed for research, experimentation, and hands-on scientific learning.

Sports Complex

World-class indoor and outdoor facilities including courts for badminton, basketball, cricket, and football. A fully equipped gymnasium with certified trainers is available for all students daily.

Auditorium

A 1,200-seat air-conditioned auditorium equipped with professional sound, lighting, and projection systems for events, fests, and guest lectures. .

Hostel

Separate, secure hostels for boys and girls with 800+ rooms, attached bathrooms, Wi-Fi, and study areas. A nutritious daily mess, common rooms, and 24/7 security ensure a comfortable stay.

Placements & Career Opportunities

CrestHill University is committed to preparing students for successful careers through industry-focused learning, practical exposure, and professional development programs. Our dedicated placement cell works closely with leading companies to create internship and employment opportunities for students across diverse fields.
